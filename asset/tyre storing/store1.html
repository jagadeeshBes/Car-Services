Correct tyre storage is essential to keeping your tyres in top-notch condition when not in use on your vehicle, ready to be installed at a moment’s notice and ready to perform.

At Mobile Tyres we offer a superb tyre storage service, allowing you to save money and keep the valuable space in your own garage or property free from clutter whilst still having access to your tyres whenever you need them.

Nowadays, many motorists switch between summer tyres and winter tyres every single year. This is a fantastic way to ensure that your car performs as well as possible all year long and is best suited to the varying road conditions as the seasons change.

When they are not in use, why not store your tyres with us in our market leading storage area where they will be kept completely dry and secure from both the elements and external threats such as theft and vandalism.

Why is Storing Tyres Such a Big Deal?

Besides saving space in your own property, why is it so important that car tyres are stored correctly when not in use?

The biggest reasons include:

Tyre Storage

Keeping tyres in the best possible condition

Poorly stored tyres can deteriorate rapidly, reducing their lifespan and even making them unsafe for use on the road. Exposure to direct sunlight, contact with chemicals, and changes in temperature are all factors that can damage the black rubber on your tyres. Store tyres in a contained environment with Mobile Tyres and we will ensure they stay dry, clean, and safe until you need them again.

Easy seasonal changes

Choosing an expert mobile tyre fitter to look after your winter or summer tyres during the months they are not required makes the process of changing them over much easier. not only will we keep your tyres safe in a convenient location so they are ready at a moment’s notice, but we can bring them to you directly and fit them on your behalf if you wish.

Save Space

Tyre Storage

Expert Tyre storage service

Mobile Tyres take pride in our tyre storage service no matter the brand or tyre size and go above and beyond to protect the tyres entrusted to us by our clients.

Cutting Edge Storage

Our storage facility is custom built to store wheels and tyres of all shapes and brands. Our tyre hotel is fully climate controlled with the humidity level tracked and kept at an optimal level at all times.

Top Notch Security

All tyres stored with Mobile Tyres are kept protected around the clock with state-of-the-art security systems including 24/7 CCTV surveillance, access control technology, and alarm units.

Unrivalled Knowledge

As we are expert tyre fitters as well as storing tyres we know exactly how to maintain and look after your wheels in the best possible way. We will also provide you with all the tips you need to properly prepare your wheels for storage such as avoiding electric motors that generate ozone and how to use a tyre brush to remove brake dust before placing the tyres in a plastic bag that has been fully cleaned using a vacuum cleaner to prevent grime and dirt build up.

Digital inventory

All tyres are tracked using a sophisticated digital inventory management system so we can locate and retrieve your tyres the moment that you need them and bring them to a location that suits and a time you select.
